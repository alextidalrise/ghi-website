## Internal linking rules

Every page must link in three directions to create a tightly connected site structure. This is essential for SEO

authority flow and for Google to understand how pages relate to each other.

##### Upward links — every page links to its parent

- Property pages link to their community page and location page
- Community pages link to their location page and country page
- Location pages link to their country page and the homepage
- Country pages link to the homepage

##### Downward links — every page links to its children

- Homepage links to both country pages and all featured locations
- Country pages link to all their location pages
- Location pages link to all their community pages and property listings
- Community pages link to all their property listings

##### Sideways links — every page links to related pages at the same level

- Location pages link to nearby or related locations (e.g. Marbella links to Estepona and Sotogrande)
- Community pages link to similar communities (e.g. Quinta do Lago links to Vale do Lobo and Vilamoura)
- Property pages show similar properties in the same community or location
- All pages link to the relevant buying guide and the partners page where appropriate
