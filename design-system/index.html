System

The digital foundation for Golf Homes International. A framework built around calm exclusivity, reflecting the prestige and serenity of southern Europe's premier golf estates.

01

Brand Ethos

A Private Club,
Not a Portal.

The site should feel like being handed a leather-bound portfolio in a quiet room. Photography speaks first. Typography frames it. Nothing is loud. The brand earns attention through restraint, not through competing for it.

Rooted in
the Landscape.

Every color traces back to the terrain: the deep green of manicured fairways, the warm sand of coastal dunes, the bleached linen of Mediterranean light. The palette is the place.

02

Color Palette

A restrained palette. White is the primary canvas; linen warms feature sections and pairs with photography. Deep Golf Green handles typography and structural weight. Sand Gold is used sparingly for accents and interactive states only.

Deep Golf Green #1F3D34

Soft Linen #F5F1E8

Sand Gold #D6C3A3

What Playfair brings

Vertical stress, larger x-height, ball terminals on the f and r. The Bold weight has a confident, "established 1962" stamp — anchoring, present, suited to a wordmark.

Trade-off: It is the most-used display serif on the luxury web. Squarespace, Etsy, and most AI-generated sites reach for it. Familiarity is its strength and its weakness.

What Cormorant brings

Slightly oblique stress, smaller x-height, taller ascenders, more extreme stroke contrast. The Light weight whispers — feels like a private hotel, not a marketplace listing.

Trade-off: Hairlines suffer below 16px on lower-DPI screens. Less recognisable at logo weight. The Bold can read closer to Playfair than you'd expect — see the wordmark above.
