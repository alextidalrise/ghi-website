Visual Identity

Design
System

The digital foundation for Golf Homes International. A framework built around calm exclusivity, reflecting the prestige and serenity of southern Europe's premier golf estates.

01

Brand Ethos

A Private Club,
Not a Portal.

The site should feel like being handed a leather-bound portfolio in a quiet room. Photography speaks first. Typography frames it. Nothing is loud. The brand earns attention through restraint, not through competing for it.

Rooted in
the Landscape.

Every color traces back to the terrain: the deep green of manicured fairways, the warm sand of coastal dunes, the bleached linen of Mediterranean light. The palette is the place.

02

Logo

The wordmark is always used in full. Two variants exist: Deep Golf Green for light surfaces, and white for dark or green surfaces. Maintain generous clear space around the logo. Never distort, recolour, or place on busy photography without a solid backing.

03

Color Palette

A restrained palette. White is the primary canvas; linen warms feature sections and pairs with photography. Deep Golf Green handles typography and structural weight. Sand Gold is used sparingly for accents and interactive states only. Charcoal, Stone Border, and Muted round out the system for secondary text, dividers, and subdued UI elements.

Deep Golf Green #1F3D34
Primary. Typography, structural borders, dark backgrounds.
Soft Linen #F5F1E8

Sand Gold #D6C3A3
Accent. Interactive states, overlines, and highlights.
White #FFFFFF
Background. Primary canvas across all surfaces; cards are defined by 1px borders.
Charcoal #2B2B2B
Secondary text. Body copy on light backgrounds, footer backgrounds, and dark UI anchors.
Stone Border #E2DED5
Dividers. Card borders, horizontal rules, and subtle separators between content zones.
Muted #7A7A7A
Subdued. Captions, metadata, placeholder text, and disabled states.

When to Use What

Primary (green) Single most important action per view. Submit, enquire, view listing. Outline (green border) Secondary action sitting next to a primary. Filter, download, learn more. Gold Editorial or accent CTA that earns emphasis: journal, membership, special collection. Used sparingly. On-dark solid (linen) Primary action on green / dark surfaces and over photography. Highest contrast. On-dark ghost Secondary on green surfaces. Pairs with on-dark solid or gold. Text link Inline tertiary navigation inside body content and card footers. Use the muted variant for back-navigation.
